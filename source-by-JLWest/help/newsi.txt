  New SI Help

  To create a new SUB Menu item:

  1. Click the New SI button.

  2. Select the Sub menu fron the
       LBSUB listbox. It will appear in 
       the LBX Listbox.
        
  3.  Select an item from the 
        Items listbox if needed.

  4.  Click the 'Execute' Button.

  5.  Enter the new tag name in the
        quey. Click the 'Ok' button.
 
  Note: The new SUB-memu tag 
     will be created .
   
      PRESS 'CLEAR' to exit .
