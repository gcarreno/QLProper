  DELETS Help

  When the system is in Delete
  mode it will allow the user to 
  delete an InfoTag or SUB-menu
  tag.

  To Delete a Main Menu Infotag:

 1. Click on the Infotag to be 
      deleted.

  2. Click the 'Delete' button.
       The Tag Name,directory will 
       be loaded into the LBSUB
       listbox and LBX listbox.
 
  3. Click the 'Execute' button. The
       selected Infotag wil be 
       deleted.

  Press '*Help to exit.

  This file delete.txt in located
  in the InstallDir\help\delete.txt.
