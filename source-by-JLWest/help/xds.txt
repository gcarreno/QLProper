  X DS Help

  When the system is in X DS
  mode it will allow the deletion
  of DataSet.

  To DeleteSet:

  1. Click the 'X DS' button.

  2. Click the DataSet to be deleted 
       in LBSub listbox.

  3. Click the 'Execute'. 

  4. Enter the 4 digit pin number.
      Click 'OK'. 

  5. Click the 'Continue' or ' 'Cancel'
       on the query.

   Press '*Help' to close.

   This operation can be aborted
   by clicking the 'Cancel' prior to
   clicking the 'Execute' button, 
   clicking the 'Cancel' on the 
   4 digit pin enquiry or clicking 
   clicking 'Cancel' on the delete
   enquiry.

 Note: If you don't know the Pin
    number enter '0000' on the 
    Pin enquiry and click 'OK'. The
    Pin will appear on the lower
    part of the form. 

      PRESS '*Help' to exit.

  This file 'X DS.txt' is located in
   InstallDir/help/xds.txt
